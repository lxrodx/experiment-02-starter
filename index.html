Kylo Ren

Born as Ben Solo, Kylo Ren is the central character in the Star Wars sequel trilogy. He turned over to the darkside in honor of his grandfather, Darth Vader. He is also the son of Leia Organa and Han solo.

Spiderman

After being bitten by a radioactive spider, Peter Parker gains powers such as web swinging, super strength, agility, and so much more. He faces many struggles and lives by the infamous saying, "With great power comes great responsibility."

The Joker

The Joker is an iconic supervillian in the DC universe. He is Batman's archenemy and is known for his chaotic nature, violence, and unpredictability.
